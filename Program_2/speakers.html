Jean-Luc Picard

"Jean-Luc Picard is a celebrated Starfleet officer, archaeologist and diplomat who served during the latter two thirds of the 24th century. The highlights of his career are centered around assignments as commanding officer of the Federation starships: USS Stargazer, USS Enterprise-D, and the USS Enterprise-E. In these roles, Picard not only witnessed the major turning points of recent galactic history, but played a key role in them also, from making first contact as captain of the Federation's flagship with no fewer than 27 alien species, including the Ferengi and the Borg. He also became the chief contact point with the Q Continuum, and served as Arbiter of Succession, where he presided over the investiture of Chancellor Gowron. Picard would expose the Romulan Star Empire as backers of his chief rivals, later aiding a Romulan underground movement of dissidents to gain a toehold on the Romulan homeworld. He continued to serve as captain of the Enterprise-E, the sixth Federation starship to bear the name, until at least 2379."
Source: Memory Alpha

Captain Picard will be at TEDxSVSU to talk about effective starship management.


Michael Scott

Michael Scott works for Dunder Mifflin Paper Company and is a businessman, regional manager, and former salesman His duties as regional manager include running the Scranton, PA branch of Dunder Mifflin, managing employees, making sure that the customers are happy, and ensuring that customers' orders are sent out on time.

Michael Scott will be at TEDxSVSU to talk about effective sales techniques for a new sales associate.
